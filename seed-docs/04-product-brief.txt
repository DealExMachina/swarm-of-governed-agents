Overview
Nexus is Acme's integrated platform for compliance and workflow automation in regulated industries (financial services, healthcare, public sector). It combines document ingestion, policy checks, and audit trails in a single tenant-deployable stack.

Claims
- Reduces manual review time by an estimated 40% in pilot customers.
- Supports SOC 2, GDPR, and sector-specific frameworks via configurable rule sets.
- First release focused on EMEA; APAC and Americas in roadmap.

Risks and limitations
- Dependency on third-party identity providers; outage or change in API can impact availability.
- Assumption: regulators do not introduce new certification requirements before our next release cycle (H1 2025).

Goals for the product
- Achieve 50 production deployments by end of 2025.
- Add at least one new vertical (e.g. energy or telecom) in 2025.

Owner: Product Lead. Last updated: October 2024.
