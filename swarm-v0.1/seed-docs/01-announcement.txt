ACME CORP — Q3 2024 EARNINGS RELEASE

Acme Corp (ACME) today reported financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2024.

Revenue for Q3 was $2.1M, up 15% year over year. The company attributed growth primarily to the launch of the Nexus product line in EMEA and stronger enterprise renewals in North America.

CEO Jane Walsh stated: "We are on track to meet our full-year targets. Our investment in R&D is paying off, and we see clear demand for our platform in regulated verticals." CFO Marcus Webb noted that operating margin improved to 12% from 9% in the prior year quarter.

The company reiterated its goal to hire approximately 20 engineers in Q4, with a focus on security and compliance. No guidance was updated; full-year revenue outlook remains $8.5M–$9.0M.
